TrueHue for Android

TrueHue is designed to make color recognition easier and more accessible for individuals with color vision deficiency. This app allows users to identify colors in real time through their device camera, providing both visual and voice feedback for better clarity.

With its simple and intuitive interface, TrueHue lets users explore colors around them, experience how different types of color blindness perceive hues, and even take quick color vision tests like the Farnsworth D-15 directly from their phone.

The app runs smoothly across most Android devices, ensuring that users can enjoy its assistive tools anytime, anywhere. Whether for learning, work, or daily life, TrueHue helps bring color understanding right to your fingertips.
